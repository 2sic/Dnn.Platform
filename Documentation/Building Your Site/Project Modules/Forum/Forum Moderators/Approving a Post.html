Approving a Post

How forum moderators and Administrators can approve a post to a moderated forum.

  1. Go to the moderator post queue. See "Navigating to the Moderator Post Queue"
  1. In the Forum column, click on a [Forum Name] link to view posts requiring moderation in that forum.

  1. Click the Approve link beside the post to be approved.

  1. Repeat Step 3 to approve additional posts.
  2. Click the Cancel button to return to the module.
