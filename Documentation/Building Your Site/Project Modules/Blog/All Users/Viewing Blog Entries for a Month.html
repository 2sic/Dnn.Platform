Viewing Blog Entries for a Month

How to view all of the blog entries made during a month on the Blog module. Any months when a blog entry has been made are listed on the Blog_Archive module in the Monthly list below the calendar.

  1. Go to the Blog_Archive module.
  2. Click on the [Month/Year] link below the calendar. Results are displayed in the View_Blog module.
